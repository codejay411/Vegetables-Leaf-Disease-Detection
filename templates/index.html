VEGETABLES DISEASE CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM

If farmers are able to detect there disease early and apply appropriate treatment then it can save lot of waste and prevent the economic loss. The treatment for different diseases are little different. So, its important that farmers accurately identified what kind of disease is there in that potato plant, tomato plant, pepper bell plant. So, here we are developed an application for farmers to detect what type of disease are in these plants.
